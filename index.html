Meet the Founder

The Vision Behind STUDIOKOMM

STUDIOKOMM was founded by Ayoub Ouh, a visionary photographer and videographer with a passion for capturing the essence of every moment. Based in Marrakech, Morocco, Ayoub and his team bring a unique blend of Moroccan heritage and modern creative techniques to every project.

Our mission is simple: to transform your vision into stunning visual realities. Whether it's a grand wedding, a corporate event, or a personal photoshoot, we ensure artistic excellence and professional delivery.
